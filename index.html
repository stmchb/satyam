Web designing

As a web designer, my responsibilities include creating visually appealing and user-friendly websites. I collaborate with clients and stakeholders to understand their design preferences and business goals. Using graphic design tools such as Adobe XD or Sketch, I craft wireframes and mockups to visualize the website's layout and structure. Once the design is approved, I translate it into actual web pages using HTML, CSS, and occasionally JavaScript to add interactive elements. Ensuring the website is responsive across different devices and browsers is a key aspect of my work. I pay attention to details like color schemes, typography, and imagery to enhance the overall user experience.
